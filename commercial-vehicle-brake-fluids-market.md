<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Brake Fluids Market consists of three main types: DOT 3, DOT 4, and DOT 5. DOT 3 fluid is widely used due to its affordability and compatibility with most systems. DOT 4 fluid has a higher boiling point, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. DOT 5 fluid, silicone-based, is primarily used in extreme conditions as it does not absorb moisture. These different types cater to the diverse needs of commercial vehicle brake systems, offering varying levels of performance and protection against brake system failure.</p></p>
