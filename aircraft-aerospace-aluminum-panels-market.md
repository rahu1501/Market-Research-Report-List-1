Forged Aluminum Alloy: These panels are produced by shaping and compressing solid aluminum billets under high temperatures and pressure, resulting in improved strength and toughness.</p><p>5. Cast Aluminum Alloy: This type of panel is manufactured by pouring molten aluminum into a mold and allowing it to solidify, resulting in complex shapes with high dimensional accuracy.</p></p>
