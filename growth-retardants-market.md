<p><p>Growth retardants, also known as plant growth inhibitors or growth regulators, are chemicals that control and restrict the growth of plants. They work by inhibiting the plant hormones responsible for stimulating growth, resulting in reduced stem elongation and enhanced lateral branching.</p><p>The growth retardants market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing need to enhance crop productivity to meet the growing demand for food due to increasing population is a major driving factor for the market.
