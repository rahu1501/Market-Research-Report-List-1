<p><p>FR-4 PCBs, also known as Flame Retardant 4, are a type of printed circuit board (PCB) made from a composite material called epoxy laminate. This composite material is formed by impregnating layers of glass fiber cloth with a flame-retardant epoxy resin, resulting in a rigid and durable substrate for PCBs. FR-4 PCBs are widely used in various industries such as automotive, telecommunications, consumer electronics, aerospace, and medical devices due to their excellent electrical insulation, heat resistance, and mechanical strength properties.</p><p>The future outlook for the FR-4 PCBs market is expected to be positive, driven by several factors.
