<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The market for miniature zinc die castings is categorized into different types. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten zinc into a die under high pressure. Vacuum die casting uses a vacuum to remove air from the die cavity before injecting molten zinc. Squeeze die casting applies additional pressure to the die cavity after injection to improve casting density. Semi-solid die casting uses partially solidified zinc to achieve higher structural integrity. Lastly, "Others" refer to any other methods or variations used in the production of miniature zinc die castings.</p></p>
