<p><p>The optical network component and subsystem market consists of active components, passive components, and optical modules. Active components include transmitters, transceivers, receivers, and amplifiers that actively manipulate and control the optical signals in the network. Passive components, such as splitters, couplers, and filters, do not require external power and enable signal distribution. Optical modules are devices that combine active and passive components into a single unit, facilitating efficient data transmission. These different market types collectively contribute to the overall growth and functioning of optical networks.</p></p>
