<p><p>The desiccant market is comprised of various types, including silica gel, activated charcoal, calcium sulfate, calcium chloride, and molecular sieves. Silica gel, a widely used type, effectively absorbs moisture due to its high porosity. Activated charcoal acts as an adsorbent, removing contaminants and moisture. Calcium sulfate provides a low-cost solution and is suitable for small-scale applications. Calcium chloride exhibits excellent moisture absorbing capabilities, making it suitable for high-humidity environments. Molecular sieves possess a high affinity for water molecules, making them ideal for moisture removal in various industries.</p></p>
