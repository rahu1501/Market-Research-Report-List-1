<p><p>The Decorative Plastic Paper Laminates market consists of three main types: Low Pressure, High Pressure, and Edge Banding. Low Pressure Laminates are used primarily for furniture and interior surfaces and are manufactured using a combination of heat and pressure. High Pressure Laminates are commonly used in countertops, cabinets, and other durable surfaces and are created by applying high pressure and temperature to multiple layers of paper with melamine resins. Edge Banding refers to decorative strips that are used to cover the exposed edges of furniture boards or panels, providing both aesthetic appeal and protection.</p></p>
