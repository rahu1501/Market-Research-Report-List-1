<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The exhaust gaskets market consists of three main types: exhaust manifold gaskets, exhaust flange gaskets, and header gaskets. Exhaust manifold gaskets are used to seal the connection between the exhaust manifold and the cylinder head, preventing exhaust leaks. Exhaust flange gaskets are used to seal the connection between two exhaust pipe flanges, ensuring a tight and leak-free joint. Header gaskets are specifically designed for high-performance engines with aftermarket headers, providing a secure seal between the headers and the cylinder heads. These gaskets are crucial components in maintaining the efficiency and performance of the exhaust system.</p></p>
