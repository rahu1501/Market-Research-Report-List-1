<p><p>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) is widely used in the industrial market due to its excellent properties. One-component LSR, also known as self-bonding LSR, can be cured at room temperature with a catalyst. It allows quick and effortless processing, making it ideal for medical and electrical applications. Two-component LSR, on the other hand, is cured by mixing two different liquid components. It offers improved precision and control, making it suitable for mold making, automotive parts, and consumer electronics. Both types of LSR possess high flexibility, heat resistance, and biocompatibility.</p></p>
