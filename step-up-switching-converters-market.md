<p><p>Integrated switch converters refer to the type of switching converters where the switch is integrated into the same package as the control circuitry and other components. They offer compact size and simplified design.</p><p>External switch converters, on the other hand, utilize a separate switch component that is not integrated into the converter package. These converters provide flexibility in terms of switch selection and can handle higher current and voltage levels. However, they require additional design considerations and wiring.</p><p>Both integrated and external switch converters are used in various applications including power supplies, battery chargers, and motor drives.</p></p>
