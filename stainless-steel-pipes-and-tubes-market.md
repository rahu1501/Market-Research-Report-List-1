<p><p>The stainless steel pipes and tubes market can be categorized into two types: seamless pipes and tubes, and welded pipes and tubes. Seamless pipes and tubes are manufactured without any seams or joints, providing better durability and higher resistance to pressure and corrosion. On the other hand, welded pipes and tubes are created by welding stainless steel plates or sheets together, resulting in a strong and cost-effective solution. Both types of pipes and tubes find extensive applications across various industries, including oil and gas, petrochemicals, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
