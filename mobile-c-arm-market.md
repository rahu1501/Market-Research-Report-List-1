<p><p>A Mobile C-arm is a medical imaging device used in surgical procedures to provide real-time X-ray imaging. Advancements in technology and increasing demand for minimally invasive surgical procedures are driving the growth of this market. Mobile C-arms provide several benefits over traditional fixed C-arms, such as portability, ease of use, and flexibility in positioning. These advantages have led to their widespread adoption in hospitals, ambulatory surgical centers, and specialty clinics.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing geriatric population and the rising prevalence of chronic diseases that require surgical interventions are expected to fuel market growth.
