Blended wood pellets are a combination of different types of wood, offering a unique and balanced flavor profile. Standard pellets are made from a single type of wood, typically oak or maple, delivering a reliable and consistent smoky flavor. These different types cater to varying preferences and cooking styles of BBQ enthusiasts.</p></p>
