<p><p>Extensional rheometer systems are used to measure the deformation and flow properties of materials under extensional stress. The market for these systems can be categorized into dynamic type, capillary type, and others. Dynamic type extensional rheometers measure the stress and strain relationship of materials under dynamic conditions. Capillary type rheometers analyze the viscosity and flow behavior of materials through a capillary tube. Other types of extensional rheometers include oscillatory and transient extensional systems, which assess the materials' deformation characteristics under various conditions. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries such as polymer processing and food processing where understanding material behavior is crucial.</p></p>
